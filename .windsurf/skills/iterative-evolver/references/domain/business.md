# Domain Adapter: Business

Assessment and evolution knowledge for business strategy and operations.

## Assessment Criteria

### Market Position
- **Market share**: What percentage of the target market is captured?
- **Brand awareness**: Recognition in target segments
- **Customer base**: Active customers, growth rate, churn
- **Revenue**: Current revenue, growth trajectory, unit economics

### Competitive Standing
- **Key competitors**: Who are the top 3-5 competitors?
- **Differentiation**: What makes the business unique?
- **Pricing position**: Premium, mid-market, or budget?
- **Distribution**: Channels and reach vs. competitors

### Strategic Health
- **Mission alignment**: Are current activities aligned with mission?
- **Resource utilization**: Are resources deployed efficiently?
- **Team capacity**: Skills, headcount, utilization
- **Innovation pipeline**: New products/services in development

### Financial Health
- **Profitability**: Margins, burn rate, runway
- **Cash flow**: Operating cash flow trends
- **Investment**: R&D spend as percentage of revenue
- **Customer economics**: CAC, LTV, LTV:CAC ratio

## Analysis Criteria

### Competitive Landscape
- Search for: `"{industry} market trends {year}"`
- Search for: `"{competitor_name} news strategy"`
- Search for: `"{market_segment} competitive analysis"`
- Search for: `"{industry} funding rounds acquisitions"`

### Market Trends
- Regulatory changes affecting the industry
- Technology disruptions
- Consumer behavior shifts
- Economic indicators relevant to the sector

### Benchmarks
- Industry standard KPIs for the sector
- Public company benchmarks (if applicable)
- Growth rate comparisons with market averages

## Planning Patterns

### Quick Wins
- Optimize pricing
- Improve customer onboarding
- Enhance marketing messaging
- Reduce operational waste

### Strategic Improvements
- New market entry
- Product/service expansion
- Partnership development
- Technology platform investment

## Health Indicator Thresholds

| Indicator | Healthy | Warning | Critical |
|---|---|---|---|
| Revenue growth | >20% YoY | 0-20% YoY | Declining |
| Customer churn | <5% monthly | 5-10% monthly | >10% monthly |
| LTV:CAC ratio | >3:1 | 1-3:1 | <1:1 |
| Cash runway | >12 months | 6-12 months | <6 months |
| Employee satisfaction | >80% | 60-80% | <60% |
