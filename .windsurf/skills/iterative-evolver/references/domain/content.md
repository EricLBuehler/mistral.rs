# Domain Adapter: Content

Assessment and evolution knowledge for content strategy and production.

## Assessment Criteria

### Content Inventory
- **Volume**: Total pieces of content by type (blog, video, social, etc.)
- **Freshness**: Average age of content, percentage updated recently
- **Coverage**: Topics covered vs. target topic map
- **Quality**: Writing quality, visual quality, accuracy

### SEO Performance
- **Rankings**: Keyword positions for target terms
- **Organic traffic**: Volume and trends
- **Backlinks**: Domain authority, linking domains
- **Technical SEO**: Site speed, mobile-friendliness, schema markup

### Engagement Metrics
- **Pageviews**: Per piece and overall
- **Time on page**: Average engagement duration
- **Bounce rate**: Percentage leaving without interaction
- **Conversion**: Content-attributed conversions
- **Social shares**: Distribution and amplification

### Editorial Process
- **Production cadence**: Content published per week/month
- **Editorial calendar**: Planned vs. actual output
- **Content lifecycle**: Creation → review → publish → update → retire
- **Team capacity**: Writers, editors, designers available

## Analysis Criteria

### Competitive Landscape
- Search for: `"{competitor} blog content strategy"`
- Search for: `"{topic} content marketing trends {year}"`
- Search for: `"{industry} most shared content"`

### SEO Landscape
- Keyword opportunity gaps
- Featured snippet opportunities
- Content format trends (video, interactive, etc.)
- Algorithm update impacts

## Planning Patterns

### Quick Wins
- Update outdated content
- Add missing meta descriptions
- Fix broken links
- Optimize images

### Strategic Improvements
- Launch new content format (podcast, newsletter)
- Build topic clusters
- Implement content personalization
- Develop distribution strategy

## Health Indicator Thresholds

| Indicator | Healthy | Warning | Critical |
|---|---|---|---|
| Content freshness | >80% updated <6mo | 50-80% | <50% |
| Organic traffic trend | Growing | Flat | Declining |
| Production cadence | On schedule | 1-2 weeks behind | >2 weeks behind |
| SEO score | >80/100 | 60-80/100 | <60/100 |
| Engagement rate | >3% | 1-3% | <1% |
