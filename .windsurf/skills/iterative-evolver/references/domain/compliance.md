# Domain Adapter: Compliance

Assessment and evolution knowledge for regulatory compliance and standards adherence.

## Assessment Criteria

### Standards Coverage
- **Applicable standards**: Which standards/regulations apply?
- **Coverage map**: Which sections/controls are implemented?
- **Gap count**: Number of unaddressed requirements
- **Evidence availability**: Documentation supporting compliance

### Audit Readiness
- **Open findings**: Outstanding audit findings
- **Remediation status**: Progress on corrective actions
- **Documentation**: Policies, procedures, evidence current?
- **Control effectiveness**: Are controls operating as designed?

### Risk Posture
- **Risk register**: Identified risks and severity
- **Control adequacy**: Controls mapped to risks
- **Residual risk**: Risk remaining after controls
- **Third-party risk**: Vendor/partner compliance status

### Regulatory Currency
- **Standard versions**: Are we on current versions?
- **Regulatory changes**: Recent changes affecting compliance
- **Enforcement actions**: Industry enforcement trends
- **Guidance updates**: New interpretive guidance

## Analysis Criteria

### Regulatory Landscape
- Search for: `"{standard_name} updates {year}"`
- Search for: `"{regulation} enforcement actions"`
- Search for: `"{industry} compliance trends"`
- Search for: `"{standard} best practices implementation"`

### Peer Benchmarks
- Industry compliance maturity levels
- Common finding patterns
- Best practice frameworks
- Certification rates

## Planning Patterns

### Quick Wins
- Close documentation gaps
- Update expired policies
- Complete evidence collection
- Address low-risk findings

### Strategic Improvements
- Implement compliance automation
- Build continuous monitoring
- Achieve new certifications
- Develop integrated risk management

## Health Indicator Thresholds

| Indicator | Healthy | Warning | Critical |
|---|---|---|---|
| Standards coverage | >90% | 70-90% | <70% |
| Open findings | 0 critical, <5 total | 1+ critical, <10 total | >1 critical or >10 total |
| Documentation currency | All current | >80% current | <80% current |
| Control effectiveness | >95% effective | 80-95% | <80% |
| Regulatory currency | Current version | 1 version behind | >1 version behind |
