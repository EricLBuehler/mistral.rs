mistral.rs handles more than text chat. Each model category has its own request shape and conventions.

- [Use vision and video input](/mistral.rs/guides/models/use-vision-input/): image and video on Qwen3-VL and Gemma 4.
- [Use image generation models](/mistral.rs/guides/models/use-image-generation/): diffusion models like FLUX.
- [Use speech models](/mistral.rs/guides/models/use-speech-models/): Voxtral for STT, Dia for TTS.
- [Use embedding models](/mistral.rs/guides/models/use-embeddings/): EmbeddingGemma, Qwen3-Embedding.

Full model and modality list: [supported models reference](/mistral.rs/reference/supported-models/).
