# Domain Adapter: Research

Assessment and evolution knowledge for research and academic work.

## Assessment Criteria

### Literature Coverage
- **Breadth**: Key papers across related fields identified?
- **Depth**: Seminal works and recent advances covered?
- **Currency**: Are sources within the last 2-3 years included?
- **Gaps**: Are known gaps in the literature identified?

### Methodology Rigor
- **Research design**: Is the methodology sound?
- **Data quality**: Are data sources reliable and sufficient?
- **Analysis methods**: Are statistical/analytical methods appropriate?
- **Reproducibility**: Can the research be replicated?

### Output Quality
- **Publication readiness**: Are outputs at publication quality?
- **Citation impact**: What is the expected impact?
- **Peer review readiness**: Has the work been reviewed?
- **Documentation**: Are methods and findings well-documented?

### Research Pipeline
- **Active projects**: Current research threads
- **Completion status**: Progress on each project
- **Collaboration**: Partnerships and co-investigator relationships
- **Funding**: Grant status and funding runway

## Analysis Criteria

### Competitive Landscape
- Search for: `"{research_topic} recent papers {year}"`
- Search for: `"{methodology} advances {field}"`
- Search for: `"{topic} conference proceedings {year}"`
- Search for: `"{research_area} grant funding opportunities"`

### Field Trends
- New methodologies gaining traction
- Emerging research questions
- Cross-disciplinary opportunities
- Funding priority shifts

## Planning Patterns

### Quick Wins
- Update literature review
- Improve documentation
- Submit to additional venues
- Strengthen methodology descriptions

### Strategic Improvements
- New research direction based on gaps
- Cross-disciplinary collaboration
- Grant applications
- Tool/methodology adoption

## Health Indicator Thresholds

| Indicator | Healthy | Warning | Critical |
|---|---|---|---|
| Literature currency | <1 year | 1-3 years | >3 years |
| Publication pipeline | >2 active | 1 active | 0 active |
| Methodology documentation | Complete | Partial | Undocumented |
| Data quality | Verified | Partially verified | Unverified |
| Funding status | Funded | Submitted | No applications |
